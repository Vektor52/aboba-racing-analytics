Safety Overview

How Safety Rating works

Safety Rating v1.5 starts from 100 points and subtracts points for invalid laps, penalties and post-race penalties.

Safety = 100 - Invalid/100 × 0.6 - Penalties/100 × 6.5 - Post-race/100 × 8.0

Clean driving bonus is added when a driver has no penalties or post-race penalties:

  • +1 point for 5+ laps
  • +2 points for 10+ laps
  • +3 points for 15+ laps

Invalid laps are already included in the formula, so the bonus rewards clean participation without requiring every lap to be valid.

Grades: S 95–100, A 85–94.9, B 75–84.9, C 60–74.9, D below 60.

Confidence depends only on total laps: very low under 20, low 20–49, medium 50–79, high 80–149, Veteran 150–284, Legend 285–374, Server Legend 🏆 375+.

Current rating uses ACC result files only. Contacts, damage events and live incidents are not included yet and are planned for v3-live.
